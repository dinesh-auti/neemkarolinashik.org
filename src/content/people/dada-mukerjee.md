While the Western devotees often tell the story of *finding* Maharaj-ji, **Dada Mukerjee** tells the story of *living* with him. A respected professor of economics at Allahabad University, Dada became one of Baba's most trusted Indian devotees, and for long stretches Maharaj-ji simply lived in his household.

His books — *By His Grace* and *The Near and the Dear* — are written with the quiet authority of someone who saw Maharaj-ji not as a distant saint but as a daily presence: demanding, playful, infinitely loving, utterly beyond ordinary understanding. For anyone who wants the texture of Maharaj-ji's actual days, Dada's writing is a treasure.
